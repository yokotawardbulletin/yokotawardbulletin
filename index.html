Announcements

Work of Salvation Announcements

Dinner with the Missionaries: Please invite your friends to meet with the missionaries and invite the Elders to have a meal in your home! Elders Jarman and Tiu can be contacted directly via facebook messenger, phone, or email. See the contact information in the missionary inforation section.

Monday Dinner and Games: Elder and Sister Gill invite you to join them on Monday Evenings at 6:00pm for dinner and games at their Fussa apartment. This invitation is extended particularly to single members or anyone whose family is elsewhere. If you would like to come, please reach out to Sister Gill in advance (so they know how much food to prepare and can give directions) via FB Messenger (Jeri Lyn Risenmay Gill) or email at jeri.gill@missionary.org

The ward has a ward temple plan! We encourage each family to prayerfully consider what steps they might take prepare their family to enter the House of the Lord. View the entire Ward Temple and Family History Plan, Here!

    "Through small and simple invitations, we can build eternal families and gather Israel on both sides of the veil."
  • Reach out to a Temple and Family History consultant to start submitting names to the temple
  • Create a Four Generation Family Tree
  • Get started on FamilySearch.org
  • Participate in Family History Activities!

Stake Missionary Preparation Classes: These are the 1st and 5th Sundays of every month. Full time missionaries teach on Fast Sundays, guest speakers from the stake will teach on 5th Sundays. See the Stake Calendar for details.

All Primary and Youth activities are held on Wednesdays from 630pm - 7:45pm at the church.

General Announcements

Choir Practice: The Ward Choir will practice immediately after church on Sundays. All current and perspective members of the choir are invited to attend.

Distribution Center: A Distribution Services store in the Tokyo Temple Annex is open by appointment only, Thursday to Saturday from 10:00am to 2:00pm. You may schedule an appintment by following this link. The store is in the basement level of the annex, and parking is available for store customers.

Parking: As a reminder, ward members may park in the paid hospital parking lot next to the church and will be reimbursed. Please use this parking only if you are unable to find parking at the church or walk from base.

The bishopric have circulated a new Photo Consent Form and would like all members to please fill out a copy of the form and turn it into a member of the bishopric either in person or via email. Thank you.

Church Cleaning Schedule: Check when your family is assigned to clean the church by CLICKING HERE. If you need keys to access the building, contact Brother Michael Moore or anyone in the bishopric.
