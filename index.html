Announcements

Work of Salvation Announcements

Updated! The Tokyo Temple will be rededicated on July 3rd, with a public open house scheduled to run from June 3rd through June 18th. Please visit the Tokyo Temple Website to view updated information and to make a reservation to attend the open house. This will continue to be updated as more information is released.

The ward has a ward temple plan! We encourage each family to prayerfully consider what steps they might take prepare their family to enter the House of the Lord. View the entire Ward Temple and Family History Plan, Here!

    "Through small and simple invitations, we can build eternal families and gather Israel on both sides of the veil."
  • Reach out to a Temple and Family History consultant to start submitting names to the temple
  • Create a Four Generation Family Tree
  • Get started on FamilySearch.org
  • Participate in Family Hisotry Activities!

Exchange food for blessings! The missionaries are once again allowed on base! Sign Up Here! and contact the missionaries directly (they can't see the signup sheet) to invite the missionaries to join you for dinner and bless your family with a message.

Stake Missionary Preparation Classes: These are the 1st and 5th Sundays of every month. Full time missionaries teach on Fast Sundays, guest speakers from the stake will teach on 5th Sundays. See the Stake Calendar for details.

Primary and Youth Announcements

All Primary and Youth activities are held on Wednesdays from 630pm - 7:45pm at the church.

General Announcements

Updated! Choir Practice: The Ward Choir will be performing for Stake Conference! Practicing will continue after church each sunday until conference, and the performers must arive at the Stake building at 9:00am on conference day. All those wishing to lend their talents are welcome (there is an especial need for more male members).

New! Steak Conference is going to be held on May 14th and May 15th. Please take the time to read the Invitation to Stake Conference and contact the families you minister unto to personally invite them to attend with their families.

We are pleased to announce the opening of a new Distribution Services store in the Tokyo Temple Annex (next to the Tokyo temple). As a result of temporary safety protocols, the store is open by appointment only, Thursday to Saturday from 10:00am to 2:00pm. You may schedule an appintment by following this link. The store is in the basement level of the annex, and parking is available for store customers.

More Parking! We are excited to notify you that ward members may now park in the paid hospital parking lot next to the church and will be reimbursed. It is asked that you only use this parking after finding no spaces available in the church parking lot. Reimbursements will be applied for Sunday parking only - submit your receipts to the Ward Financial Clerc of a member of the Bishopric via a photo sent by email to Brother Russ Reese or via paper receipts with your last name written at the top of each one. Reimbursements will be processed on amonthly basis.

The bishopric have circulated a new Photo Consent Form and would like all members to please fill out a copy of the form and turn it into a member of the bishopric either in person or via email. Thank you.

Church Cleaning Schedule: Check when your family is assigned to clean the church by CLICKING HERE. If you need keys to access the building, contact Brother Michael Moore or anyone in the bishopric.
