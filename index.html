Message from the Bishop

February - So we are one month down and eleven more to go in 2021. Looking at our Ward Mission plan we finished with a solid 151 invitations, which is a great start! But remember, the Lord does not expect us to feed the 5000 ourselves, we just have to provide some bread and fish and He will do the rest.
