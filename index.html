Bishop's Corner

Message from the Bishop

You are the Women He Foresaw, a mothers day message from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

May - I believe our ward has some of the most amazing women in the whole church. The spirit of service and love that I see in all of you is remarkable. Mother’s Day is a special holiday in that everyone has a mother, either on this side or the other side of the veil. Take the time to reach out to those special women in your life and say thank you. I am grateful for the mothers and women of this ward, you are instrumental in the work of the Lord. If I could have one wish today it would be that women could see themselves as Christ sees them. Please know you are genuinely loved and appreciated.

- Bishop Nathan Larson

Primary Announcements

Bishop Larson's Challenge

Starting from today, read 7 days without missing a day and I will give the primary kids a piece of candy on Sunday every week they complete the challenge. If they can’t have candy I can also do a small 3D printed prize. I’m trying to help the primary kids form a habit of daily personal scripture reading. Also 1 verse is the minimum, if they are already reading more than that then don’t slow down or read less, just read AT LEAST 1 verse everyday no matter what. If you miss a day then start again the next day but try to keep your streak alive.
