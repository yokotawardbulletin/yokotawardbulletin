Tokyo Temple Re-Dedication: As the Tokyo Temple finishes up its renovations, we are reminded again of an opportunity to re-evaluate our preparedness to enter the Lord’s House. We invite everyone to prepare for the Tokyo temple re-dedication and re-opening. Let’s start with getting our temple recommends up to date and preparing spiritually to enter the Lord’s house again. Despite COVID-19 restrictions, temple recommend interviews can take place by phone or “Zoom”. Contact your bishopric for more details.

    How to get your Patriarchal Blessing during COVID-19
  1. Bishop/Breanch Pres. interviews member & issues recommend using Patriarchal Blessing Submission System.
  2. Bishop/Breanch Pres. prints out or emails the recommend for the member.
  3. Member requests an appointment with Patriarch Nibley by email or telephone (contact info is on recommend).
  4. Brother Nibley schedules an online meeting to get acquainted and review the recommend.
  5. Brother Nibley typically gives the blessing in the conference toom on the first floor of the Area Office Building in Hiroo: 5 Chome 10-30 Minamiazabu, Minato City, Tokyo 106-0047
    • Precautions are excersised, such as washing hands and wearing masks.
    • A limited number may be present or observe remotely, no video or audio recordings allowed.
    • Sisters should be accompanied by someone, such as a family member or close friend.
