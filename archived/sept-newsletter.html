Tokyo Temple Re-Dedication: As the Tokyo Temple finishes up its renovations, we are reminded again of an opportunity to re-evaluate our preparedness to enter the Lord’s House. We invite everyone to prepare for the Tokyo temple re-dedication and re-opening. Let’s start with getting our temple recommends up to date and preparing spiritually to enter the Lord’s house again. Despite COVID-19 restrictions, temple recommend interviews can take place by phone or “Zoom”. Contact your bishopric for more details.

Church Policy and Communication

New WiFi Network @ Church Buildings: The password for accessing the new network is “alma3738”

Church Magazine Changes: Three church magazines will serve Church members worldwide.

New Gospel Voice: Use Alexa for spiritual content. Try commands like "Alexa, ask Gospel Voice to read this week's lesson" or "Hey, Google, ask Gospel Voice to play hymn number 30."

Children and Youth Protection Training: All adults who work with children or youth are to complete the course and to renew the training every three years.
